## Design Context

### Users
General consumers — everyday people holding an insurance policy (health, auto, home) who need help cutting through the legal language. They're often mildly anxious: they pulled out their policy because something happened or they're not sure what they're actually covered for. They're not financially sophisticated. They want plain answers fast, with no condescension.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy. Precise. Calm.

The interface should feel like a knowledgeable friend who's read the whole document so you don't have to — not a flashy tech product. It earns trust through restraint, not assertion.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Minimal & focused.** Every element earns its place. No decorative chrome, no colored card grids, no gradient text.

**Visual tone:** Clean editorial — think well-designed reference material. Warm off-white background (not harsh white), near-black text, deep forest green as the sole accent color. Fraunces (variable optical serif) for headings; DM Sans for body. Generous whitespace with tight typographic rhythm.

**Theme:** Respects system preference (light/dark). Light mode is primary — the app is reading-heavy and documents live in the light.

**Anti-references:** No cyan-on-dark "AI tool" aesthetic. No colorful emoji cards. No glassmorphism. No modal dialogs. No rounded cards with thick colored left-borders.

**The one thing users remember:** It feels like someone organized the chaos of your policy into a clean, readable brief — then stayed to answer your questions.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity first** — if a choice is between visually interesting and immediately readable, choose readable.
2. **Trust through restraint** — fewer colors, fewer animations, less noise. Confidence comes from precision, not decoration.
3. **Document-native** — the UI should feel appropriate for reading and understanding a text document, not filing a support ticket.
4. **Responsive without amputating** — the chat panel stacks below on mobile; all functionality remains accessible.
5. **Purposeful motion** — one smooth entrance animation on results reveal; nothing decorative.
